Is it allowed to spay my Cat?

Question 4: : Is it allowed to spay my Cat?

Is it permissible to spay a cat if there is a medical reason, such as the possibility of urinary tract diseases or viruses due to its inability to mate?

Answer:

In the name of Allah, all praise is due to Allah, and may Peace and Blessings be upon the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ).

If the cat has any problem, disease, or pain in the body, then it is allowed to spay the cat. Because this will be considered as a benefit for the cat. But if there is no pain, and you want to spay the cat for no reason at all, then it is not allowed. And Allah knows best.
