Vision

To build a comprehensive, easy-to-navigate encyclopaedia of Islamic knowledge—rooted in the Qur’an, the Prophet’s Sunnah, and the methodology of the righteous predecessors (Salaf).

Mission

Ask Sunnah is an educational & da‘wah platform offering clear, academic answers supervised by Dr. Sheikh Falah Kurkully (PhD in Hadith Sciences). We welcome questions on Islamic, social, and personal matters from Muslims and non-Muslims alike.

Aims

  • Spread the message of Islam and call people to its beauty.
  • Dispense authentic knowledge and dispel ignorance.
  • Answer contemporary needs with evidence from sacred texts.
  • Refute misleading arguments against Islam.
  • Offer practical advice on daily issues through scholarly guidance.

Methodology

We adhere to the beliefs of Ahl as-Sunnah wa’l-Jamaa‘ah and follow the scholars of the Salaf as-Saaliheen. Every answer:

  • Is based on the Holy Qur’an and authentically-narrated Sunnah (Saheeh).
  • Reflects the views of the four Imams: Abu Haneefah, Maalik, ash-Shaafa‘i, and Ahmad.
  • Is cross-checked with contemporary fiqh councils and leading Hadith research.
  • Avoids fruitless debates, focusing instead on beneficial, evidence-based guidance.

About Dr. Sheikh Falah Kurkully

Dr. Sheikh Falah Kurkully is a distinguished Islamic scholar with a PhD in Hadith Sciences. He has studied under some of the most esteemed scholars of our time, including Sheikh Ibn Uthaymeen, Sheikh Salih Al-Fawzaan, Sheikh Abd Al-Muhsin Al-‘Abbad, Sheikh Muqbil bin Hadi Al-Wadi‘i, and Sheikh Abu Bakr Al-Jazairi. With decades of experience in da‘wah and education, Dr. Kurkully brings deep insight, clarity, and scholarly integrity to every response featured on Ask Sunnah.

O Allah, make us steadfast on the path of guidance, help us adhere to what pleases You, and accept our humble efforts, Ameen.
